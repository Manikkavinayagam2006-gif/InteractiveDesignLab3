Our Campus

🌳 Green Environment

Spread over 50+ acres of lush green landscape, our campus provides a serene and eco-friendly atmosphere ideal for learning and growth.

📚 Central Library

A state-of-the-art library with over 50,000 books, digital journals, IEEE access, and quiet study zones for students.

🔬 Advanced Labs

Fully equipped laboratories for each department including AI Lab, Robotics Lab, VLSI Lab, and Research Centers.

🏠 Hostels

Separate hostels for boys and girls with modern amenities, Wi-Fi, mess facility, and 24/7 security.

🏀 Sports Complex

Indoor and outdoor sports facilities including cricket ground, basketball court, gym, and yoga center.

🍽️ Cafeteria

Hygienic multi-cuisine cafeteria serving nutritious meals, snacks, and beverages at affordable prices.

🚌 Transport

Fleet of 30+ buses covering major routes across Pudukkottai, Trichy, Madurai, and surrounding areas.

🏥 Medical Facility

On-campus medical center with qualified doctors, first-aid, and tie-ups with nearby hospitals for emergencies.
