About Our College

📜 Our History

Sethu Institute of Technology (SIT), located in Pudukkottai, Tamil Nadu, was established in 1995 under the auspices of the Sethu Educational Trust. Affiliated to Anna University, Chennai, the institute has been a beacon of technical education for over two decades.


With a vision to nurture competent engineers and innovators, SIT has consistently delivered quality education, research, and holistic development to thousands of students across India.
